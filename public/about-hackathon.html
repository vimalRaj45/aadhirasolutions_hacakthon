General Rules & Guidelines

  • Team Size: Teams must have exactly 4 members. Only 1 member acts as Team Leader and represents the team.
  • Problem Selection: Teams must pick exactly 1 problem statement from the 8 official selections.
  • Development: Plagiarized projects or pre-built commercial tools are strictly disqualified. Open-source tools/libraries are allowed.
  • Entrance Check-in: The approved QR entry ticket sent to the leader's email is mandatory for physical verification at the check-in desk.
