Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can a team consist of fewer than 4 members? +

A: No. The Aadhira Solutions Hackathon requires teams of exactly 4 members (1 leader and 3 members). All team lineup input sections are mandatory to complete step 2.

Q: How long does the verification take? +

A: Once you submit your payment proof, our coordination committee reviews it. Approved teams will receive their entrance check-in ticket with QR code in their email within 12 hours.

Q: Can I change my selected problem statement later? +

A: Problem statement selections are locked once submitted. In case of an absolute error, please fill in the contact support form on this page with your Team ID to request a modification.

Q: What is the file size limit for receipt uploads? +

A: The receipt upload file size must be at most 2MB. Only image file formats (PNG, JPG, JPEG) are accepted.
